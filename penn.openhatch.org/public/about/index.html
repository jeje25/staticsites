Who we are

The event is put together by Asheesh Laroia, Yuvi Masory, and Felice Ford with the generous help of the Penn Department of Computer and Information Science. Asheesh hopes that it becomes the first of a series of OpenHatch-led student immersion events in the free software community.

Sponsors

We're grateful to have the sponsorship of Github, Bitbucket, O'Reilly, and Pragmatic Bookshelf. They have provided us with funding and lots of goodies to give to attendees.

Get in touch

If you want to get in touch with us, the best thing to do is to email us. Try this: penn@openhatch.org

Want to chat ahead of the event? Ask questions on ##penn on FreeNode, below. Consider using IRC your first open source accomplishment :)
